<p><strong><u>Grading Scheme for Examinable Common Curriculum Subjects</u></strong>
</p>
<p>With effect from 2025, a new grading scheme will be implemented for examinable
common curriculum subjects at lower secondary level. These subjects are
Art, Design &amp; Technology (D&amp;T), and Food &amp; Consumer Education
(FCE).</p>
<p></p>
<p>Under this new system, student progress and skills will be assessed using
a four-level grading scale.</p>

<p><strong><u>Grade Mapping Table</u></strong>
</p>
<p>Grade mapping is only used when there is a need to compute aggregate scores
using grades of subjects taken at more demanding levels for purposes of
post-secondary progression, e.g. ELMAB3 for admission to Year 2 of Higher
Nitec (HN) and Polytechnic Foundation Programme (PFP), ELR2B2 for polytechnic
admissions.</p>
