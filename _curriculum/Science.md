<h3>Vision</h3>
<p>Gessians with strong foundation and interest in Science</p>
<h3>Mission</h3>
<p>To develop Gessians with innate curiosity and critical thinking skills</p>
<h3>About</h3>
<p>Our department is dedicated to fostering a lifelong love for scientific
inquiry. We design our programmes to meet the diverse interests and learning
needs of all Gessians, ensuring everyone is inspired to explore the world
around them.</p>
<p>Our key initiatives include the Applied Learning Programme (ALP), Vermicomposting,
and Edible Gardens, which encourage environmental stewardship and sustainability
through hands-on green projects. To cultivate critical and creative thinking,
as well as robust information and communication skills, we offer platforms
under our school’s Capstone Programme to stretch Gessians' potential through
the Singapore Junior Olympiads (Physics, Chemistry, and Biology) and the
International Competitions and Assessments for Schools (ICAS) Science Competition.</p>
