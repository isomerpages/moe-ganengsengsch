<p>On behalf of all staff and students, we welcome you and your child/ward
to our Gessian family! Your child/ward is now part of a well-established
school that has produced Gessians of Excellence, Standing and Significance.
Please note that students <strong>do not</strong> need to report to their
posted secondary schools upon receiving their S1 posting results.</p>
<p>We have uploaded all the relevant information for the Secondary 1 (2025)
e-Registration on this webpage. Please refer to the attached documents
and links.</p>
<h4><strong><u>(1) Online Information Form</u></strong></h4>
<p>Secondary 1 Registration will open at <strong>8:00 am on Thursday, 19 December 2024</strong>.&nbsp;
You can access the link <a href="https://go.gov.sg/gesssec1registration2025" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://go.gov.sg/gesssec1registration2025</a> &nbsp;to
complete the Online Information Form to <strong>confirm your child’s/ward’s registration</strong> as
a student of GESS.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 25%;" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Sec_1_Registration_QR_Code.png">
</div>
<p>The online registration will <strong>close at 12pm the same day</strong>.</p>
<p>If you intend to transfer your child/ward to another school, you will
still need to register him/her with us first by completing the Online Information
Form. You may then proceed with the transfer process to another school.</p>
<p>Our Online Information Form will also help us to collate other information
we will need for administrative purposes like your child’s/ward’s medical
condition (if any), cohort T-shirt size, whether parents are on-board Parents
Gateway (PG) and a short feedback on the registration process. We would
also like you to indicate on the form if you would be attending the Meet-Parent-Session
on <strong>10 January 2025, Friday, 5 pm</strong>.</p>
<p>Parents can also use this platform to ask any question(s) they may have
after our e-Registration webinar.</p>
<h4><strong><u>(2) Secondary 1 (2025) e-Registration Webinar</u></strong></h4>
<p>To share with you some of the key information about the school as well
as administrative matters pertaining to the registration process, we will
be conducting a <strong>Zoom Webinar at 8.30 am on 19 December 2024</strong>.
There will also be a talk by our Senior School Counsellor, Mr Sim Kwang
Mong, on how to support your child/ward in his/her transition to a Secondary
School. Details of the webinar will be sent to parents via PG on 18 December.</p>
<h4><strong><u>(3) Class Allocation</u></strong></h4>
<p>Details of the Secondary 1 class allocation will be sent to all parents
via <strong>PG </strong>by <strong>19 December 2024, 8 am.</strong>
</p>
<p>For parents who are not on-board PG, your child’s/ward’s Form Teacher
will contact you to inform you of your child’s/ward’s class after the e-Registration
webinar.</p>

<h4><strong><u>(7) First Day of School Programme</u></strong></h4>
<p>All Secondary 1 students are to reach school and report to the Arena (school’
parade square) by<strong> 7.30 am on 2 January 2025, Thursday</strong>.</p>
<p>They are to engage in silent reading while waiting for the morning assembly
to start.</p>
<p>The reporting attire will be school uniform with white short / green skirt.
Students who have yet to purchase their GESS school attire may report in
their primary school uniform.</p>
<p>All students are to bring along the following items:</p>
<p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; an appropriate story
book for the Silent Reading Programme;</p>
<p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; notebook and writing
materials;</p>
<p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; thermometer for temperature
taking exercise; and</p>
<p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; GESS school badge (for
submission to Form Teachers).</p>
<p>The first day of school programme will be sent via <strong>PG</strong> by <strong>27 December 2024.</strong>
</p>

<h4><strong><u>(10) Parent-Teacher Association (PTA) Membership</u></strong></h4>
<p>All parents/guardians are automatically members of the GESS PTA. The school
will be making a one-time collection of $24.00 based on an annual subscription
of $6.00 per year on <strong>8 January 2025</strong>. The amount collected
will be used to support school activities like the supervised self-study
programme for students.</p>
<p>Students with older siblings in GESS would only be required to make payment
for the remaining years they have left in GESS upon graduation of their
older siblings. (e.g. if a parent has an older child in Secondary 4, 2025,
he/she will only need to make a payment of $18.00 for the remaining three
years in GESS for the younger child, i.e. for 2026 to 2028.)</p>
<p>The school hopes to promote greater parental involvement in school activities
as well as PTA related programmes. Kindly complete the PTA registration
form via the link: <a href="https://go.gov.sg/gessptaregistration2025" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://go.gov.sg/gessptaregistration2025</a>
</p>

<h4>&nbsp;<strong><u>(13) Road Safety</u></strong></h4>

<h4><strong><u>(14) School Smart Card (SSC)</u></strong></h4>
<p>All students entering Secondary 1 will be issued a new Secondary Level
SSC. While waiting for their new SSC to be ready, Secondary 1 students
can continue to use their Primary Level SSC for concessionary travel till <strong>end of April 2025</strong>.
Secondary 1 students can purchase the Bus Concession Pass (BCP) using their
Primary Level SSC but they will need to purchase the BCP for <strong>secondary school students</strong>.</p>
<h4><strong><u>(15) Photo-taking for Secondary 1 Students</u></strong></h4>

cockpit system. The 8 passport-sized photographs will cost $5.00 and the
money is to be paid directly to the photographer on the day itself.</p>
<p>More information on the date of the photo-taking will be provided at a
