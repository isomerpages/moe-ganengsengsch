<p>GESS drama club proudly presented <em>Alice by Heart</em>, a musical inspired
by Alice’s Adventures in Wonderland set during World War II’s London Blitz,
where a girl named Alice used her favorite book to cope with her best friend
Alfred’s terminal illness. Thank you parents for your strong support today.
It meant a lot to the students. Onward Gessians.</p>
