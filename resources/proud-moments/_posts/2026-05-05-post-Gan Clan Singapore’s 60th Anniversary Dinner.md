<p>Gan Eng Seng School was honoured to join Gan Clan Singapore’s 60th Anniversary
Dinner, graced by Guest of Honour Minister Gan Kim Yong. Our Dance Ensemble
lit up the evening with a vibrant performance, showcasing the passion and
spirit of our Gessians.</p>
<p>For six decades, Gan Clan Singapore has strengthened bonds and contributed
meaningfully to our multicultural society — nurturing youth, supporting
families, and building a caring community. Here’s to a shared legacy of
standing strong in our roots and uplifting others.</p>
<p>Congratulations on this milestone!</p>
