<p>Our Sec 2 cohort officially begun their 4D3N Outdoor Adventure Learning
Camp on Wednesday!</p>
<p>Wasting no time, students dove straight into Day 1 with a series of exciting
outdoor activities under beautiful weather. The energy was high, spirits
were strong, and the adventure is just getting started! We can’t wait for
more fun, challenges, and unforgettable memories ahead!</p>
<p>Day 2 kept the momentum going with even more enriching experiences</p>
<p>Half of our Sec 2 Gessians embarked on a coastal exploration, learning
about local flora and fauna while deepening their appreciation of the natural
environment. The other half took on thrilling outdoor activities such as
zip lining, rock climbing, kayaking, and outdoor cooking. It was another
meaningful day filled with discovery, teamwork, and learning beyond the
classroom.</p>
