<p>On 4th March, Sec 1 students receive their Personal Learning Devices (PLDs).</p>
<p>During the session, students were guided through the purpose of the PLD
programme and how the devices will support their learning in the classroom.
Teachers also shared important guidelines on responsible digital use.</p>
<p>The highlight of the day was the unboxing moment, where students eagerly
opened their PLDs and began setting them up for the first time. This marks
an exciting step in their secondary school journey as they embark on a
more interactive and technology-enabled learning experience.</p>
