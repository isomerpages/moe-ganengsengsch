<p>Gan Eng Seng School was honoured to be invited to attend the Henderson-Dawson
National Day Carnival on 24 August 2024, Saturday at Delta Sports Centre.</p>
<p>GESS was represented by our National Cadet Corps (NCC), Dance Ensemble,
Marching Band and Entrepreneur Club. Our NCC unit and Marching Band was
also involved in the Parade observance ceremony while the Dance Ensemble
and Entrepreneur Club put up a stage performance and game booths respectively.
Our service to the community indeed!</p>
