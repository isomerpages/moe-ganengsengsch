<p>On 25 February, our Secondary 3 Higher Chinese students stepped up as
Student Guides at Gan Clan Singapore, leading a heritage tour for Secondary
2 students.</p>
<p>Through creative storytelling, they shared the clan’s history and the
lasting legacy of philanthropist Gan Eng Seng, bringing Chinese heritage
to life beyond the classroom.</p>
<p>An authentic learning experience that builds confidence, language skills,
and cultural appreciation</p>
