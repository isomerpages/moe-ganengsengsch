<p>Our Secondary 3 Pure Geography students recently embarked on an exciting
Geographical Investigation at Merlion Park!</p>
<p>Through this hands-on fieldwork experience, students applied a variety
of geographical investigation methods, including conducting land use surveys,
pedestrian counts, crafting survey questions, and interviewing tourists.
Beyond developing their inquiry and data collection skills, the experience
also encouraged meaningful collaboration as students worked together to
delegate responsibilities and support one another throughout the investigation.</p>
<p>Most importantly, the fieldwork provided students with valuable opportunities
to build their confidence in communicating with members of the public while
deepening their understanding of real-world geographical concepts beyond
the classroom.</p>
