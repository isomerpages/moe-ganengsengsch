<p>What did the graduating cohort of Gessians learn during the Gessian Experience
Week? Through their industrial visits and Worldskills conference, they
better understood the world of work and the relevant skills required for
the future workforce. They also learnt about their own talents, interests
and aspirations. Sincere appreciation goes to the Polytechnics and JCs
that joined us and also to our ECG Counsellor, Ms Christina Ho. To all
our industrial partners, thank you!</p>
