<p>On 6 April 2026, Mishael D/O Haja Mydin (3-4) and Aydin Matin bin Mohammed
Junaidi (3-5) proudly attended the Appointment Ceremony for Rakan Bahasa
(Friends of the Malay Language).</p>
<p>Their certificates of appointment were presented by Zhulkarnain Abdul
Rahim, Chairman of Bulan Bahasa 2026 (Malay Language Month) and Minister
of State at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and Ministry of Social and
Family Development.</p>
<p>A proud moment as our students take on the role of advocating and promoting
the Malay language within the community. Onward!</p>
