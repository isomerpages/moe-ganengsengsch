<p>Our Sec 1–4 students had a great time at the Singapore Coffeeshop Heritage
Gallery</p>
<p>As part of the Cultural Performance Exposure Scheme (CPES), a lively cross-talk
performance brought local coffeeshop culture to life with humour, witty
dialogue, and lots of laughter</p>
<p>A fun and meaningful way to experience our heritage!</p>
