<p>Sec 4 and 5 students embarked on the GESS Resilience Walk as part of their
final day of GESS Experience Week. They were accompanied by school leaders
and teachers. It was a moment to reflect on their journey; the challenges
faced, the growth achieved, and the resilience built along the way.</p>
<p>As they take their next steps forward, they carry with them not just memories,
but strength, unity, and the confidence to face what lies ahead.</p>
<p>Onward, Gessians!</p>
