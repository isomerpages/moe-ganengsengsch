<p>Congratulations to our GESS Dance Ensemble for earning the Certificate
of Distinction at the Singapore Youth Festival (SYF) Arts Presentation
in the International Dance category! Their captivating performance of Steel
and Grace: The Soldier’s Journey was a powerful blend of strength, storytelling,
and artistry — an experience they embraced with passion and pride. A heartfelt
thank you to our dedicated dancers, instructors, and teachers for their
unwavering commitment and the incredible heart they poured into every step
of this journey.</p>
