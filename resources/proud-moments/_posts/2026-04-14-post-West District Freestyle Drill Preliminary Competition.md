<p>On 30th March, our NCC Freestyle Drill Team took on the West District
Freestyle Drill Preliminary Competition.</p>
<p>Months of hard work and precision paid off as they clinched 5th place
in the preliminary competition, and secured their spot in the Grand Finals!</p>
<p>Catch them in action on 16th April (afternoon) at OCBC Square as they
bring the heat once again!</p>
