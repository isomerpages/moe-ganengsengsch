<p>GESS 'O'Level Preparatory Workshop 2025 was organized by Gan Eng Seng
School Tamil Language Unit for Sec 4E/5NA and 4NA (SBB) students. The workshop-cum-hands-on
sessions, offered tips on answering GCE ‘O’ Level questions effectively.
GESS thanks CHIJ St Theresa's Convent and Outram Secondary School for their
active participation.</p>
