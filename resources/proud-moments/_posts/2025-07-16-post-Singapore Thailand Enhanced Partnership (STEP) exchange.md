<p>Singapore-Thailand Enhanced Partnership (STEP) exchange at GESS on 11/7/25
with Suankularb Wittayalai Bangkok School.</p>
<p>Students were involved in D&amp;T experience, classroom immersion, lunch,
dialogue with Principal and CCA scouts experience. Our teachers who participated
in previous STEP exchanges also shared fond memories with Mr Tanon and
Mr Zen.</p>
