<p>Day 1: We kicked off our Guangzhou journey at the Guangdong Museum exploring
the city’s rich history and cultural heritage.</p>
<p>A highlight was the lifelike jade-carved chicken, symbolising prosperity
and the Cantonese saying, “No Feast Without Chicken.”</p>
<p>A meaningful and memorable start to our adventure!</p>
<p>Day 2: A day packed with learning, culture and fun! We visited Huangpu
Experimental School Affiliated with Hunan Normal University, experienced
student life in China, and explored their robotics programme from robot
combat to brainwave-controlled cars</p>
<p>We also made our own dim sum, visited the Five Rams Statue and Chen Clan
Academy, and ended the day at bustling Beijing Road Pedestrian Street</p>
<p>Day 3: We became farmers for a day at the rooftop edible garden, harvesting
green beans, guavas, cucumbers and more! In our cultural lesson, we tasted
lychees and turned their seeds into spinning top races, plus some Cantonese
singing</p>
<p>We explored the Guangdong Science Centre before ending with the spectacular
Guangdong Eternal Love show with stunning effects, animations, and horses
charging into the theatre!</p>
<p>Day 4: We said our goodbyes to our tour guides and wrapped up our Guangzhou
trip. Grateful for the opportunity to learn, explore, and grow a journey
filled with laughter and memories that will stay with us</p>
