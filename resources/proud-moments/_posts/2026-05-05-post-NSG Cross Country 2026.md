<p>On 28 April 2026, our runners participated in the NSG Cross Country 2026.
A huge congratulation to Ng Xin Hao Asher (2-5) for clinching 17th place
in the C-Division Boys race, with a timing of 13min 58s. This marks the
end of our season and the last race for our Secondary 4s. We are proud
of each and every runner for their hard work, discipline and determination
throughout this season.</p>
<p>We thank our teachers in charge, Mr Tan Yong Geng, Mrs Chan, Ms Chong,
Mr Seah and Mr Lim for your endless support. Additionally, our appreciation
to Coach Pandian for your continued guidance. Onward!</p>
