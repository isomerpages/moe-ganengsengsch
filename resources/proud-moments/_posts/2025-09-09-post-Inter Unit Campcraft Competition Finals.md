<p>On 30 August 2025, our NPCC team of 11 cadets competed in the Inter-Unit
Campcraft Competition Finals. Within 15 minutes, they built a location
marker and two survival gadgets using only ropes and poles. Demonstrating
skill, teamwork, and resilience, they proudly secured 5th place amid tough
competition. Congratulations to our NPCC cadets for the excellent teamwork,
creativity and discipline displayed!</p>
