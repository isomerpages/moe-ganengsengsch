<p>Welcoming our friends from Indonesia</p>
<p>We are delighted to welcome our friends from <em>SMPK Penabur Jakarta</em> on
their first school exchange visit to Gan Eng Seng School.</p>
<p>It was our pleasure to host Ms Debbie Paskawaty, Principal, together with
teachers Ms Hani Pertiwi and Ms Anggraeni Wahyu Sartika, as well as their
students from two of their 17 campuses.</p>
<p>This visit marks the beginning of a meaningful partnership — a wonderful
opportunity for cultural exchange, shared learning, and new friendships
between our schools. We look forward to many more collaborations ahead
as we journey together in nurturing globally minded learners.</p>
<p>SMPK Penabur Jakarta, welcome to GESS!</p>
