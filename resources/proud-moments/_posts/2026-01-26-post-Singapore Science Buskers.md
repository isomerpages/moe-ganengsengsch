<p>Singapore Science Buskers is a unique programme that brings science to
the streets. Last year, our team comprising Zenon Teo Zhe Wei from 4-4,
Muhammad Amir Bin Baharudeen from 3-3 and Nguyen Quang Hai from 2-1 emerged
as one of the Finalists.</p>
<p>Ng Jing Hong from 4-4 also clinched a Bronze award at the World Science
Olympiad 2025 and is ranked in the top 10% of all the participants in 2025.</p>
<p>Onward Gessians!</p>
