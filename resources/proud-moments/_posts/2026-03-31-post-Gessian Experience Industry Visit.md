<p>Our 4-3 students had the privilege of visiting the SBS Transit Seletar
Bus Depot as part of their Gessian Experience Industry Visit</p>
<p>It was an eye-opening experience as they learned how buses in Singapore
are operated and maintained, and gained insights into the lives of Bus
Captains and the many individuals working behind the scenes.</p>
<p>We also discovered a bus enthusiast among us - Isaac Yu, who impressed
everyone with his knowledge by answering many of our bus-related questions!</p>
<p>Curious about buses? Feel free to chat him up!</p>
