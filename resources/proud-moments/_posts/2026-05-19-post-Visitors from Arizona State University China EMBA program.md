<p>We played host to a very special group of guests—delegates from the Arizona
State University (ASU) China EMBA program!</p>
<p>We had a fantastic afternoon filled with meaningful exchanges:</p>
<p>An insightful look into Singapore’s education landscape: “Multiple Pathways,
Multiple Possibilities.” We also explored our campus and got a hands-on
taste of our vibrant CCA experiences!</p>
<p>And a sharing on GESS’s 140-year legacy.</p>
<p>A huge thank you to all our guests for the wonderful energy and cross-cultural
inspiration. We hope to see you again soon!</p>
