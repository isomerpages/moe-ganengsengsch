<p>GESS NPCC celebrates achieving the Distinction Award for UOPA 2025!</p>
<p>This achievement is more than just a title. It’s a reflection of the heartwork,
resilience, and unwavering dedication of every individual in our journey.
From our supportive school leaders and teacher officers, to our committed
adult volunteers (HO, SCI, CI), dedicated cadets, and friends of NPCC.
This award belongs to all of you.</p>
<p>Here’s to celebrating this proud moment together! Onward!</p>
