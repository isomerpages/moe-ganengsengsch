<p>On 12 September, Gan Eng Seng School participated in the World Robotics
Olympiad 2025 Singapore Nationals held at Ngee Ann Polytechnic. We are
pleased to announce that the team comprising Carla Andres Goldstein (3-4),
Mohandas Isacc Valeriano(3-5) and Sean Tan Zi Jing (1-7), emerged as runner-up
in the Future Innovators Seniors (14 to 19 years old) category. Congratulations!!!</p>
