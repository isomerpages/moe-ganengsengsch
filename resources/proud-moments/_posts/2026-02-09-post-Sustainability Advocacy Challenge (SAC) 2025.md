<p>When our youth lead, sustainability follows. Proud Silver Award winners
for Sustainability Advocacy Challenge (SAC) 2025!</p>
<p>Our Entrepreneur Club (E-Club) has done us proud. The SAC is a student-oriented
movement aimed at providing both student voice and agency to youths in
Singapore to do their part in advocating for environmental sustainability.
The competition aims to create awareness about sustainability. We conducted
an in-house workshop in school and at Henderson Crescent RN to teach residents
about making eggshell fertiliser.</p>
