<p>On Saturday, 25 April, the GESS Drama Club’s efforts culminated in a 3rd
place finish at the Singapore Finals of the International Science Drama
Competition (Open Category). The competition drew theatrical talents from
secondary schools and junior colleges across the nation, bringing together
students who are keen on exploring the intersection between science and
the performing arts. Their performance, “False Frame”, tackled the dangers
of AI deepfakes, a timely and thought-provoking take on this year’s competition
theme, AI See The Future. We couldn’t be prouder of our Dramarians for
representing GESS with their creativity and conviction. Onward!</p>
