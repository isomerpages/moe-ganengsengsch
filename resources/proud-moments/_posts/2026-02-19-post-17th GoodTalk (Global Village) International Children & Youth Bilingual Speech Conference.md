<p>On 10 February, two of our students participated in the 17th GoodTalk
(Global Village) International Children &amp; Youth Bilingual Speech Conference,
organised by the GoodTalk Children &amp; Youth Cultural Communication Center.</p>
<p>During the event, youths from different countries and regions explored
a common theme through bilingual collaboration in Chinese and English.
Participants engaged in discussions, delivered speeches, shared insights,
and showcased their talents on an international platform.</p>
<p>We are proud to share that Yao Nan Xi (4-4) won the First Prize, while
Bliss Foo Xinrui (4-5) was awarded the Third Prize</p>
