<p>At Telok Blangah Community Resilience Day (19 July 2025), we recognise
Dylan Goh from GESS St John Brigade for his brave and timely use of CPR-AED
during a cardiac arrest incident. His quick action made a real difference
to a life that was saved. We are indeed very proud of Dylan. Onward!</p>
