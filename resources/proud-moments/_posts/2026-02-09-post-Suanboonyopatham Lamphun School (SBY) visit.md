<p>We’re delighted to welcome our friends from Suanboonyopatham Lamphun School
(SBY) back on our campus</p>
<p>From last year’s exchange in Thailand to today’s joyful reunion, this
visit celebrates friendship, cultural exchange, and shared learning. We’re
grateful for the meaningful connections and excited to continue strengthening
the bond between our schools.</p>
<p>Welcome back, SBY!</p>
<p>“I’m glad to have had the opportunity to discuss and consult together
on education, the use of technology, and collaboration in the school’s
work.</p>
<p>I hope we will have more opportunities to meet again and exchange educational
experiences in the future.”</p>
<p>-Mr Pradit Wangpruek (Principal)</p>
