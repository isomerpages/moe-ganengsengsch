<p>Dragon Scouts celebrated Founder’s Day today by reciting the Scout Promise
and Scout Law, reaffirming their commitment to the values and principles
of the Scout Movement.</p>
<p>Every year on 22 February, Scouts around the world come together to observe
Founder’s Day — a time to remember Robert Baden-Powell and the timeless
vision he set in motion when he founded the Scout Movement.</p>
<p>More than a commemoration of the past, this day is a renewal of our promise
— a commitment to make the world a better place, beginning with ourselves,
serving our communities, and contributing to our nation.</p>
