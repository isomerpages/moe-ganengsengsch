<p>Our Sec 4 Gessians stepped beyond the classroom and into the real world
through 7 exciting Learning Journeys!</p>
<p>From NCS to SBS Transit, Singapore Navy, AETOS Security, Singapore Retailers
Association, Singapore Polytechnic, and Anabelle Psychology. Students got
a firsthand look at different industries and career pathways.</p>
<p>Engaging with professionals</p>
<p>Seeing knowledge come alive</p>
<p>Thinking about real-world challenges</p>
<p>These experiences helped our students build key 21st Century Competencies.
Students were given the opportunity to strengthen their Communication skills
through questioning and dialogue, deepen their Civic Literacy by understanding
the role of organisations in society, and exercise Adaptive and Inventive
Thinking.</p>
<p>Meaningful. Eye-opening. Inspiring.</p>
<p>A powerful step towards shaping their future aspirations!</p>
