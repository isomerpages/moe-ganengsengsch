<p>Fresh from the Home of Athletics at Kallang, Ng Xin Hao Asher (2-5) delivered
an incredible performance in the 800m C Division Boys Final, clinching
3rd place with a timing of 2:15!</p>
<p>The journey doesn’t stop here! Asher now sets his sights on the 1500m
Final next Wednesday at the National Stadium, while the rest of our Cross
Country CCA runners continue to train hard for the 4x400m relay.</p>
<p>Both races will take place on 15 April at the National Stadium. Stay tuned!</p>
