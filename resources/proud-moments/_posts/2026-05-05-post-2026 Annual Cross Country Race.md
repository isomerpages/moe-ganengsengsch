<p>Neither a morning drizzle nor a damp track could dampen the GESS spirit!</p>
<p>As the clouds cleared, we were greeted by a stunning rainbow, the perfect
backdrop for our Annual Cross Country Race. Our Gessians showed true resilience
and integrity, pushing through every kilometer and giving their absolute
best.</p>
<p>We also commemorated International Friendship Day with mini game booths,
celebrating the importance of building harmonious relationships within
our community and beyond.</p>
<p>This event was more than just a race. It was a celebration of the collective
effort from our parents, staff, and students. Together, we lived out our
core values of Excellence, Friendship, and Respect.</p>
<p>Here’s to the memories made and the spirit that keeps us going strong!
Onward!</p>
<p>Thank you @gess_ifc for the amazing photos!</p>
