<p>Indian Educators from Himachal Pradesh visited GESS on 9 April 2024. Through
our exchanges, we learnt much about each other's school systems, cultures
and traditions. What resilience students in Himachal Pradesh show when
they walk more than 10 km to school everyday!</p>
