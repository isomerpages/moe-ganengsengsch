<p>Day 1: We kicked off our trip with a milk tea workshop, where we learnt
the traditional Hong Kong-style method using a silk stocking and even made
egg tarts. The treats turned out delicious and we enjoyed them before dinner.</p>
<p>Day 2: We rode the iconic Ding Ding Tram and enjoyed spectacular views
of Hong Kong from the upper deck before taking the Peak Tram to Victoria
Peak for sightseeing and shopping. We also visited the Hong Kong Science
Museum and Space Museum, where interactive exhibits and an Omnimax show
made learning fun. The day ended with a walk along Tsim Sha Tsui Promenade
and Avenue of Stars, featuring famous celebrities’ handprints and the iconic
Bruce Lee statue.</p>
<p>Day 3: We received a warm welcome at Man Kwan Pak Kau College and toured
their school facilities that support STEM/STEAM learning. We also experienced
their school culture during recess. Later, we visited the Hong Kong Housing
Society Exhibition Centre and the Hong Kong Heritage Museum to learn more
about Hong Kong’s housing, culture and history.</p>
<p>Day 4: On our final day, we joined classroom lessons at Man Kwan Pak Kau
College and experienced how students in Hong Kong learn English, Science
and ICT. The friendly teachers and active students made it a meaningful
cultural exchange and a memorable conclusion to our Hong Kong trip.</p>
