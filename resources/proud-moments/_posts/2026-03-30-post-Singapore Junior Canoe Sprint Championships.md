<p></p>
<p>Kudos to our canoeists!</p>
<p>Our team paddled their hearts out at the Singapore Junior Canoe Sprint
Championships (SJCC) from 14–16 March and we’re proud to celebrate three
medal finishes!</p>
<p>Aydin Matin Bin Mohamed Junaidi – 1st place</p>
<p>U15 Div 2 Men K1/JK1 500m</p>
<p>Anson Teu Zhi Shan – 3rd place</p>
<p>U15 Div 2 Men K1/JK1 500m</p>
<p>Chau Cheng Feng – 3rd place</p>
<p>U16 Div 2 Men K1/JK1 500m</p>
<p>Amazing effort, grit and determination on the water!</p>
<p>A big thank you to our teachers and school leaders for the constant support.
Onward Gessians!</p>
