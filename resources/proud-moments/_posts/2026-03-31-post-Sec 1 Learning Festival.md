<p>Sec 1 Learning Festival at the Science Centre sparks the joy of learning
through hands-on, real-world experiences. From coding and digital skills
to cultural appreciation and dance, students explore beyond the classroom
while growing holistically.</p>
<p>Through engaging STEM workshops like strawberry botany in jam-making,
ice-cream chemistry, rocket engineering, forensic investigation, electronics,
and fragrance creation, students bring science to life and connect concepts
to real-world applications.</p>
<p>More than just learning, the learning festival strengthens class and cohort
spirit as students collaborate, problem-solve, and create meaningful shared
experiences.</p>
