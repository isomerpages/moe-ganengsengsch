<p>Day 1: We started our Chiang Mai adventure with an early morning flight
from Singapore</p>
<p>After arriving, we enjoyed a hearty local meal before visiting the Chiang
Mai Cultural Centre and the 3 Kings Monument, where we learned about the
city’s rich history. We ended the day with a Khantoke Dinner and an exciting
cultural performance featuring our very own Gessians!</p>
<p>Day 2: We visited Suanboonyopatham Lamphun School for the first day of
our School Immersion Programme joining lessons, meeting our buddies, and
touring the large campus. In the afternoon, we customised umbrellas at
the Umbrella Factory before visiting the Golden Temple and enjoying a Mookata
dinner.</p>
<p>Day 3: Back at Suanboonyopatham Lamphun School, we performed an energetic
dance during assembly and took part in cultural activities such as banana
leaf craft, music, and dance. After a final lunch and farewell with our
buddies we visited the Long Neck Village and Elephant Poo Park where we
made bookmarks from recycled elephant paper. We ended the day with dinner
at Kahomao-Khaofang Imaginary Jungle a Michelin Guide-recommended restaurant.</p>
<p>Day 4: On our final day, we visited the Silver Temple and made DIY keychains
as souvenirs. After lunch at a local halal restaurant, The Baragus, our
tour guides treated us to authentic ChaTraMue milk tea before we flew back
to Singapore.</p>
<p>Through this memorable trip, we forged new friendships, gained cultural
insights, and created lasting memories. We would like to thank all the
teachers who made this amazing experience possible.</p>
