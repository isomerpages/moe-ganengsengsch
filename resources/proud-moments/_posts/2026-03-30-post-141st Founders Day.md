<p>141 Years of Gessian Legacy</p>
<p>Gan Eng Seng School commemorated our 141st Founder’s Day with the theme
“Leaning In, Lifting Up, Looking Out.” Together with our Guest of Honour,
Tan Cheng Kiong, we celebrated the enduring vision of our founder, Gan
Eng Seng, whose belief in education continues to inspire and shape generations
of Gessians.</p>
<p>Our heartfelt appreciation goes out to our valued partners — the School
Advisory Committee (SAC), GESSOSA, Parent-Teacher Association (PTA), community
partners, senior Gessians, parents, staff and students — for being part
of this meaningful celebration.</p>
<p>The day concluded with school-wide Values-in-Action activities, as our
Gessians leaned in to serve, lifted others up, and looked out to make a
difference in the community. (See our next post for more highlights.)</p>
<p>Leaning In. Lifting Up. Looking Out</p>
<p>Onward, Gessians!</p>
