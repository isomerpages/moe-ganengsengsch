<p>2025 Racial Harmony Day commemoration was a collaboration among NE, MTL,
SJB and PTA. GESS RHD aimed to promote a deeper understanding/ conversations
among our students on the different ethnic groups in Singapore. Looking
at the activities and lessons planned, we have achieved our objectives!
Thank you, parents for your strong support! Onward Gessians.</p>
