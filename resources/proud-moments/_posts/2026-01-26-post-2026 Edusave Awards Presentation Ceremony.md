<p>We are incredibly proud of Mithani Florence Firoj (4-3) and Kim Mai Louis
Piff (4-7), who did a fantastic job emceeing the Edusave Awards Presentation
Ceremony organised by Henderson-Dawson Citizens' Consultative Committee
on the evening of 21 January 2026.</p>
<p>Both students took the stage with poise and confidence, engaging the audience
with ease. They also had the privilege of having a warm conversation with
Ms Joan Pereira, Adviser to Tanjong Pagar GRC Grassroots Organisation.</p>
<p>Well done, Florence and Kim!</p>
